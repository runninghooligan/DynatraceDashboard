GOAL 2 · INCIDENT REDUCTION PREVENTION LAB

25% YoY reduction · Dynatrace p-cards + AlertSite + threshold monitoring + recurring patterns
LIVE PROTOTYPEBack to Command Center
Recommended Goal 2 Framing25% YoY

Reduce incidents by 25% year over year by lowering avoidable incident creation from Dynatrace p-cards, AlertSite synthetic failures, threshold-monitoring noise, duplicate event clusters, and recurring known patterns. The dashboard should monitor incident-generation signals in real time and analyze historical trends to identify tuning, grouping, automation, playbook, problem-management, and severity-aware triage opportunities. P3 Non-Major should be treated as a prevention/triage signal, not just noise: support teams may be called quickly depending on application and scope, and these events are a key early-warning layer for preventing P3 Major, P2, or P1 escalation.

Derived Goal Objectives
1 Reduce low-value auto-closed incidents

Identify incidents auto-closed within 15 minutes, with no work notes, no reassignment, and no customer impact.

2 Lower p-card to incident conversion

Monitor p-cards approaching incident criteria and resolve, group, or suppress known patterns before INC creation.

3 Reduce synthetic-generated noise

Analyze AlertSite failures by monitor, location count, duration, and correlation with Dynatrace.

4 Target repeat offenders by severity

Rank recurring incidents by VCT, app, entity, monitor, root cause, assignment group, error signature, and P1/P2/P3 Major/P3 Non-Major severity path.

5 Use automation and playbooks

Find known patterns with safe remediation actions and track estimated incidents avoided.
